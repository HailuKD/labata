How does Labata work?

  • Read & Share Stories

    Browse a wide range of inspirational stories about Oromo people, places, events, and history. Share stories that motivate you and contribute to Oromo literature.

  • Contribute Your Story

    Submit your own inspirational or motivational story, topic, or visual that you believe can inspire others and enrich Oromo heritage.

  • Access Creative Services

    Contact Labata for professional design, printing, photography, videography, and website services to help you tell your story or promote your brand.

Frequently Asked Questions

Labata is a digital magazine and creative service platform focused on sharing inspirational stories and visuals about Oromo people, places, events, and history. We also offer design, printing, photography, videography, and website services.

You can submit your story, topic, or visual through our website. We welcome contributions that inspire, motivate, and add value to Oromo literature and culture.

Labata provides design and printing (T-shirts, banners, magazines, brochures, and more), as well as photography, videography, and website design services for individuals and organizations.

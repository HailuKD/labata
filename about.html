The Story Behind Labata

A platform dedicated to celebrating Oromo culture and creativity.

Our Purpose

Our Mission

To be the leading digital voice for the Oromo community by publishing inspirational stories that preserve our heritage and celebrate our identity. We aim to enrich Oromo literature and provide a platform for new and established voices to share their perspectives.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Oromo stories are globally recognized and accessible, inspiring pride and connection among generations. Labata strives to be a bridge between the past and the future, fostering a vibrant cultural landscape through creative expression and community engagement.

Digital Magazine

At the heart of Labata is our commitment to storytelling. Our digital magazine, published in Afan Oromo, is a space for authentic narratives about Oromo people, places, and history. We believe every story has the power to inspire, educate, and unite our community.

Creative Services

Beyond storytelling, we empower our community with professional creative services. From graphic design and printing to photography and web development, we provide the tools individuals and businesses need to bring their visions to life and make a lasting impact.
